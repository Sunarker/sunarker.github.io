Reseach Group

Bo Han, Feng Liu and I are faculties in the machine learning area. Although we have different research focus in subareas, we share the same vision that we should make AI trustworthy in intrinsic design, efficiency and interpretation etc., which motivates us to build up the Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning (TMLR) Group to promote the collaboration and development.
