About me

Jiangchao Yao is currently a tenure-track assistant professor in Cooperative Medianet Innovation Center, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Before that, Dr. Yao has been a researcher in Data Analytics and Intelligence Lab, DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group. He received his joint PhD degree in Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Ya Zhang and University of Technology Sydney under the Supervision of Ivor W. Tsang. He got the bachelor degree in South China University of Technology.

My research targets to develop industrial machine learning algorithms applied to the general real-world applications. Currently, I focus on the robustness and privacy issues of machine learning algorithms.
